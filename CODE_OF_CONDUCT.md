# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving constructive feedback
- Helping other contributors
- Respecting different opinions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam or abusive language

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove inappropriate contributions.
